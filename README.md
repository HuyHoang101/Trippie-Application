**Trippie** is a social travel platform designed to connect travelers and simplify trip planning through collaboration. Instead of planning alone, users can find teammates, share tasks, and manage their journeys together.

### 1. Discovery Feed
* **Find Your Squad:** A dedicated feed for "Trips looking for Teammates."
* **Trip Previews:** View trip destinations, timelines, and recruitment status.
* **Join Requests:** Send requests to organizers to become part of their journey.

### 2. Collaborative Workspace (My Trips)
* **Shared To-Do List:** Real-time collaboration where every member can comunication about create, edit, or delete trip tasks.
* **Item-Level Discussion:** Integrated comment sections for each task to keep discussions organized.
* **Team Management:** Trip owners can review applicant profiles and manage team members.

### 3. Profile & Reputation System
* **Traveler Identity:** Showcase your past trips and experiences.
* **Trust & Ratings:** A star-rating system to build a reliable community of travelers.
* **Post-Trip Reviews:** Rate your teammates after completing a journey together.
